About the Museum

Guardians of Labo's Heritage

Located in the heart of Camarines Norte, Museo de Labo serves as the primary custodian of the municipality's historical artifacts, cultural relics, and artistic heritage.

From ancient indigenous roots to the Spanish colonial era and the rich mining history of the region — every piece tells a unique story waiting to be discovered.

Our Story

Preserving Heritage for Generations

Dedicated to documenting, preserving, and sharing the cultural legacy of Labo, Camarines Norte.

Mission & History

Founded in 2026, the Museo de Labo began as a digital initiative to document and preserve the rich cultural history of our region.

Our mission is to provide an accessible platform where students, historians, and enthusiasts can explore artifacts that define our shared human experience.

We believe history should be interactive and inclusive. Through our digital exhibits, even the most fragile artifacts can be studied and appreciated by all.
